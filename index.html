My Political Ideology

I am an Anarcho-Communist, and as such I host books, images, links, and various documents about Anarchist Communism and related ideologies and ideas. I find that the state is unnecessary for happiness and a coordinated society, and that it is conducive with oppression. I belive in freedom and equality for all - regardless of race, sex, and sexual or gender orientation. I oppose all ideologies focused on the state, conducive hierarchies, nationalism, etc. (essentially I am against every authoritarian ideology - left wing or right wing). This means I am against Fascists, Nazis, Liberals, Libertarians / Anarcho Capitalists, Marxists, Maoists, as well as Conservatives and both political parties of the United States - although Republican is (in my opinion) the worse of the two. I am against Capitalism, as it results in severe inequality between worker and CEO, as well as the exploitation of the lower class.


For those with criticism, I suggest reading the following books - each hosted on this site's library. For those with questions about the practicality of Anarchist Communism or those who are looking for examples of Anarchist ideas in practice, I suggest reading Anarchy Works by Peter Gelderloos. Other books I recommend reading are The Conquest of Bread and Mutual Aid, both written by one of the most influential Anarchists (if not THE most influential) - Pëtr Kropotkin.

Software

I believe in free as in freedom software in all aspects. Free software meaning software that the user has control over, not the other way around. You should be able to use a program to satisfy your needs - meaning you should be able to modify and redistribute it as you see fit, so long as you keep it free software. You can read more about free software here.


I personally use Gentoo Linux with almost entirely free software. I highly recommend Gentoo for those wishing to expand upon their Linux knowledge. I also suggest configuring and compiling your own kernel - not only to learn but to have a more lightweight sytem.


I use suckless software, such as dwm, st, and dmenu. I use dwm with my own custom status program mentioned below. note that my dwm configuration is custom tuned for my custom keyboard layout.


I use a keyboard layout called Colemak. I switched on 11/14/21, and so far I have made quite significant progress. I did not switch to become faster - but to save my wrists in the long term as I do a lot of typing. I highly recommend Colemak for those who get wrist pain while typing or for those who suffer from RSI or arthritis.
