anarchist resources

foreward

since most anarchists are also socialist/communist (like myself), most of their work is released into the public domain or under copyleft licenses like the gplv2. this makes it incredibly easy to access and read anarchist texts.

resources

the anarchist library
popular texts - anarchist library
anarchist faq - anarchist library
libcom.org - many pages on anarchist communism
anarcho communism - libcom.org
list of anarcho-communist resources - /r/anarchocommunism

note: most of these resources contain various other works, which i highly suggest reading. don't be fooled by common misconceptions about anarchism and actually read some things. i suggest starting with anarchy works by peter gelderloos, as it is a fantastic introduction to anarchism and does a great job at debunking many of these common misconceptions.
