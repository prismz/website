A Simple Journal Program in Haskell

< Posts

3 Jun, 2022

A while back, I began journaling. I liked the idea of keeping journals digitally, since I didn’t have a proper journal at the time.

All of the typical note-taking programs people usually use were too much for what I wanted to do. I didn’t need a full office suite or image support or cloud storage. I just needed a way to organize a directory of text files.

I looked and didn’t really find any programs I liked, so I just wrote my own, partly as a Haskell exercise.
